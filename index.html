Message from the Principal

"Dear Parents, Students, and Staff,

As the Principal of our esteemed school, I am honored to address you all through this school website. With an unwavering commitment to providing a nurturing and stimulating environment, we strive to lay the foundation for a lifelong love of learning in our young learners.

At our pre-school, we firmly believe that every child is unique and possesses immense potential waiting to be unlocked. Our dedicated team of teachers, support staff, and administrators work tirelessly to create an atmosphere that promotes holistic development, fostering cognitive, social, emotional, and physical growth.

We emphasize on Academic Excellence, Character Building, Social and Emotional Development, Creative Expression, Parent Partnership, and Safety and Well-being. We recognize the importance of fostering a strong academic foundation right from the early years. We firmly believe that education is a shared responsibility between the school and parents. We encourage parents to actively participate in their child's education journey. Regular communication channels, parent-teacher meetings, workshops, and family events help create a strong bond between home and school, facilitating a holistic approach to child development.

In conclusion, our pre-school is a place where children thrive, grow, and embark on their educational voyage with joy and enthusiasm. We remain committed to providing an enriching experience that lays a solid foundation for their future academic and personal success. Thank you for entrusting us with the responsibility of nurturing your child's early years. Together, let us continue to foster a love of learning and create a brighter future for our young learners."

Warm regards,

Mr. Achyut Dahal
Principal
